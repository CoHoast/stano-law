About Paul J. Stano

30+ Years Dedicated to Protecting Ohio Families

Your Trusted Elder Law Attorney

For over three decades, Paul J. Stano has dedicated his practice to helping Ohio families navigate the complexities of elder law, estate planning, and Medicaid planning. His mission is simple: to provide compassionate, expert legal guidance that protects your family's future.

As a member of the National Academy of Elder Law Attorneys (NAELA), Paul brings extensive experience to every case. He understands that legal planning isn't just about documents—it's about protecting the people and assets you've worked a lifetime to build.

Paul is also a published author, having written extensively on Medicaid planning, estate planning, and asset protection strategies specific to Ohio families. His books have helped thousands of families understand their options and make informed decisions.

What sets Paul apart is his personal approach. He takes the time to understand each family's unique situation, providing personalized guidance rather than one-size-fits-all solutions. His goal is to ensure you feel confident and informed every step of the way.
